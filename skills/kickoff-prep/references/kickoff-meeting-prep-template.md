## Strategic Questions (Use During Kickoff)

### MVP Definition & Priorities
- What are the 3 non‑negotiable features for v1?
- What can be deferred to v2 without harming core value?
- Do we prioritize polish over breadth, or breadth over polish?

### Users & Validation
- How many beta users and how will we recruit them?
- How will we measure success for v1?
- Which metrics matter most initially and why?

### Privacy & Data
- Which data will we not collect under any circumstance?
- How do we balance privacy with functionality (e.g., recommendations)?
- What anonymization level is required for any public lists/data?

### User Experience
- Walk through the ideal flow from open → confirm an action.
- What minimum info is required during onboarding?
- What happens when an invite/request expires or is ignored?

### Content/Curations (if applicable)
- How will we seed initial content/places?
- Can users add new items or select from curated lists only?
- What is the minimum data model per item?

### Go‑to‑Market
- Will we launch in a single city/segment first?
- How will we acquire early users beyond friends and network?
- Any partnerships or collaborations to explore?

### Collaboration & Decisions
- Who decides on product vs. technical trade‑offs?
- How should updates be delivered and how often?
- What level of involvement is desired in technical decisions?

### Integrations & Limitations
- Which integrations are essential in v1?
- Any legal or technical limitations to consider?
- Is any platform explicitly out of initial scope?
